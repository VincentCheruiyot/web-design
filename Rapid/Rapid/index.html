About Us

Globalfora Research Group (GRG) is an international group registered by the government of Kenya, registration certificate No. PVT-PJUL3P8. Its membership comprises researchers from different parts of the world, with diverse interests and disciplines in education, administration, engineering, community development, tourism and hospitality, project management, statistics, social work, psychology, human resource management, Nutrition and public health, guidance and counseling, Business leadership and management, Designing, Assessment and evaluation, Information and Communication Technology, project planning.

Our group is guided by the following emblems:

Vision

Leading in Innovative Research, Publications, Consultancy, Education and Training.

Mission

To build synergy for innovative research, publication, consultancy, education and training for sustainable development

Philosophy

Globalized research and innovation for sustainable development.

Team Bio

Globalfora Research Group Experience:


Prof. Lazarus Ndiku Makewa

Founder, Director and Chairperson

Prof. Ndiku holds a PhD in Educational Communication and Technology from Moi University, Kenya. He is Professor of Educational Communication and Technology. Formerly was department chairperson and School Dean at Baraton University, He was School Dean, Deputy Vice Chancellor Academics and acting Vice Chancellor, Lukenya University. He has been Deputy Vice Chancellor Academics at Rwenzori International University, Uganda. Prof. Ndiku is the founder, CEO and Editor in Chief of Journal of Research Innovation and Implications in Education (JRIIE), Founder and proprietor of Center for Research Implications and Practice. He is a renowned researcher and a practitioner. He has authored, co-authored and published in peer reviewed journals. He has edited and authored books among them: IT Issues in Higher Education: Emerging Research and Opportunities; Theoretical and Practical Approaches to Innovation in Higher Education; Technology Supported Teaching and Research Methods for Educators; Handbook of Research on Enhancing Teacher Education with Advanced Instructional Technologies, among others.

Mr. Esau has reviewed manuscripts in the Journal of Research Innovation and Implications in Education and also reviewed book chapters and manuscripts and has attended international research conferences both at national and international levels. Frequently Asked Questions

Some of the common questions we receive

  • Is your team competent with proven track of record?

    Our team comprises of experts in their own disciplines, which makes our products and services competitive and long lasting.

  • Can you also help on effective report findings ?

    Majority of our members have been in research for decades, making their reporting characterization unique and acceptable to our clients.

  • Can I consult you on any research related service?

    We provide all research services to our clients, whether they are students, companies, organizations or government entities. For example, we can help in the following stages of a research: Problem statement, research objectives, research questions, hypothesis, etc., literature review, research methodology, developing data collection tools, data collection, data analysis, data reporting, and publishing reports in manuscripts, books and in our journals.

    Our pricing is tailored towards the client’s need. We offer competitive pricing for our services and products. It is the quality of the service that determines the price.

    Not yet. We are in the process of putting operational structures in place. As soon as we are done with this process, our schools will be operational.
